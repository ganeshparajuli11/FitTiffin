Daily Rules

Simple
  • Only 3 main meals a day. No random snacking.
  • Drink 2.5 to 3 liters of water.
  • Walk at least 30 to 40 minutes daily.
  • Tea or coffee with no sugar, or very little sugar.
  • Momo or chowmein maximum once a week only.
  • At lunch take either rice or roti, not both together.
  • Dinner should be lighter than lunch.
